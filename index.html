Frequently asked questions.
Do I need to pay before my application is reviewed?
No. You apply first, get approved, and then set up payment. Owners are always free. Contractors and suppliers pay only after approval.
What does the $49 job award fee cover?
When an owner awards a job to a contractor, a one-time $49 platform fee is charged to the owner. Posting, browsing, and receiving bids are always free. This keeps DockWork sustainable while keeping owner access free.
How does the founding member offer work exactly?
Month 1 is completely free — no charge. Months 2–13 are billed at 50% off ($89/mo for contractors, $49/mo for suppliers). Month 14 onward is the standard rate. Your Gold Charter badge is permanent regardless of future subscription changes.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Subscriptions are month-to-month and can be cancelled anytime. Your Charter badge remains on your profile permanently even if you pause or cancel.
What happens if my license lapses?
DockWork monitors license and insurance status annually. If credentials lapse, your account is suspended until renewed. Your profile and history are preserved — you can reactivate once credentials are current.
Is DockWork available outside Florida?
We're launching with a focus on the Southeast and Gulf Coast — FL statewide — but approved members from all coastal states are welcome. National expansion is planned for later in 2026.
