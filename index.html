For Owners & Marina Operators
Post your project.
Receive qualified bids.

DockWork takes the friction and guesswork out of finding qualified marine professionals. Post once, receive competitive bids from pre-screened contractors, and compare every proposal side by side — with full visibility into licensing, insurance, ratings, and experience.

Step
1
Describe your project
Post an RFI to scope your project with expert input before committing, an RFP to collect formal competitive bids, an RFQ for specific pricing on defined work, or flag an Emergency for urgent storm damage or critical repairs. Upload drawings, photos, and specs directly to your posting.
Takes less than 10 minutes
Step
2
Receive verified bids from matched contractors
DockWork notifies matched contractors based on specialty, geography, tier, and availability. You can also invite specific verified contractors directly. Bidders cannot see each other's proposals — protecting the integrity of every bid from start to finish.
Blind bidding · Full transparency for you
Step
3
Compare and award with complete confidence
Every bid arrives with the contractor's verified credentials, star rating, completed job count, specialty match score, license status, and insurance details. Compare proposals side by side, ask questions directly on-platform, and award the job — all without a single phone call.
No guesswork. No phone tag.
Step
4
Track progress from start to finish
Once awarded, your project dashboard keeps everything organized. Photo logs capture before, during, and after documentation. All messaging stays on-platform. Every job has a complete, clear record when the work is done.
Built-in photo log & on-platform messaging

Frequently asked questions.
Do I need to pay before my application is reviewed?
No. You apply first, get approved, and then set up payment. Owners are always free. Contractors and suppliers pay only after approval.
What does the $49 job award fee cover?
When an owner awards a job to a contractor, a one-time $49 platform fee is charged to the owner. Posting, browsing, and receiving bids are always free. This keeps DockWork sustainable while keeping owner access free.
How does the founding member offer work exactly?
Month 1 is completely free — no charge. Months 2–13 are billed at 50% off ($89/mo for contractors, $49/mo for suppliers). Month 14 onward is the standard rate. Your Gold Charter badge is permanent regardless of future subscription changes.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Subscriptions are month-to-month and can be cancelled anytime. Your Charter badge remains on your profile permanently even if you pause or cancel.
What happens if my license lapses?
DockWork monitors license and insurance status annually. If credentials lapse, your account is suspended until renewed. Your profile and history are preserved — you can reactivate once credentials are current.
Is DockWork available outside Florida?
We're launching with a focus on the Southeast and Gulf Coast — FL statewide — but approved members from all coastal states are welcome. National expansion is planned for later in 2026.
